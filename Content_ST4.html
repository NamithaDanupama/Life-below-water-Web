Plastic pollution is one of the biggest threats to marine life. By reducing plastic waste, we can prevent harmful materials from entering the ocean.

  • Switch to reusable bags, bottles, and containers.
  • Avoid single-use plastics like straws and cutlery.
  • Choose products with minimal or biodegradable packaging.

Overfishing and destructive fishing methods harm marine ecosystems. Choosing sustainable seafood helps protect ocean biodiversity.

  • Opt for seafood that is responsibly sourced.
  • Check for certifications like MSC (Marine Stewardship Council) or ASC (Aquaculture Stewardship Council).
  • Reduce consumption of overfished species.

Beach cleanups help remove plastic waste and other pollutants from coastal areas, preventing them from harming marine life.

  • Organize community efforts to remove trash from shores.
  • Dispose of waste properly to prevent it from reaching the ocean.
